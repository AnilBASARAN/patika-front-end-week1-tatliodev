Cubes of chocolate

Chocolate and pistachio flavored tasting treats...
Chocolate cubes made with only 4 ingredients are served in colorful papers or skewered. Chocolate cubes, especially included in crowded tables,
can also be served at special events such as birthdays.

Recipe by: Max Payne

Serving Size: 4 servings Preparation Time: 30 Minutes Cooking Time: 10 Minutes

Ingredients for Chocolate Cubes Recipe..

  • 350g dark chocolate
  • 150ml cream
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 150g pistachios (can also be hazelnuts or almonds)

How to Make Chocolate Cubes?

  1. Melt the dark chocolate using a double boiler.
  2. Once melted, add cream and butter to the chocolate and mix well. Take the pot off the heat.
  3. Pound the pistachios lightly in a mortar and divide them into small pieces.
  4. Add the crushed pistachios to the chocolate mixture and stir.
  5. Pour the prepared chocolate into a square aluminum mold if available, otherwise into a small tray lined with parchment paper.
  6. Refrigerate for 2 hours.
  7. Remove the chocolate from the mold and cut into square pieces.
